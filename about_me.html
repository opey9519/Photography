My Story

I am a self-taught photographer, mentored by my daughter-in-law, Tessa of Holson Photography I have always been a creative mind and have never been without at least one artistic outlet. Photography was a private obsession when my kids were growing up and I am finally chasing the dream. I am passionate about creating new feelings of appreciation, excitement, encouragement and pride. Photos inspire by evoking emotions and I am here for it!
