MEET THE PHOTOGRAPHER

I've changed a lot through the years. I even went from a strong dislike of cats, to Cat Mom of 5. One thing about me remains constant- I must have a creative vibe to all I do!

I've participated in theatre, singing, drawing/painting but photography is my favorite!

I love capturing moments in time, to be treasured. Sights, sounds reimagined at a glance.

I am passionate about creating new feelings of appreciation, excitement, encouragement, pride.

Photos inspire, ignite feelings and I am here for it!
